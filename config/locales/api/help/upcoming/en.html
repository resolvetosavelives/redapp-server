Getting started

Watch a video

%{thumb_demo_svg}

Learn how to use Simple in 5 minutes.

Who to record in Simple?

Use Simple to record every patient who has hypertension or who is taking medicines for high BP.

When the patient returns for follow-up, search for the same patient and enter a new BP.

Step 1Take patient's BP

Take an accurate BP: feet on floor, back against chair, no talking.

Step 2Write in BP Passport

If you have BP Passports, write the patient's name. Then, scan it using the Simple app.

Step 3Register patient

If they are new, register the patient.

Step 4Enter BP in Simple

Do not round the BP reading.

Step 5Enter medicines

Enter the patient's current hypertension medicines.

Step 6Save the patient

Hit SAVE and schedule the patient's next visit. Write that date on the BP Passport.

Missed visitWhat happens next?

If your patient misses their visit, Simple will send a text message reminding them to come back.

Tip

Always make sure to record the patients at the correct facility. On the home screen, always check the facility in the top left.

Register a patient

There are two ways to register new patients:

Option 1If you have BP Passports

  1. Press this button on the main screen
    %{help_scan_bp_passport_svg}
  2. Pick up a blank BP Passport and scan the QR code.
    %{help_bp_passport_svg}
  3. Search for the patient’s full name.
  4. If you do not see the patient in the search results, tap Register as a new patient.
  5. Fill the registration form.
  6. Talk to your patient and then complete the medical history.
  7. Record the patient visit, press Save.

Option 2If you do not have BP Passports

  1. Press Enter patient’s name
  2. Enter your patient's full name.
  3. If you do not see the patient in the search results, tap Register as a new patient.
  4. Fill the registration form.
  5. Talk to your patient and then complete the medical history.
  6. Record the patient visit, press Save.
%{help_tip_svg}

Tips

  • Always make sure to record the patients at the correct facility. On the home screen, always check the facility in the top left.
  • Enter mobile number if you can!
  • If you cannot find a follow-up patient, it is okay to register them as a new patient.
  • If a patient loses their BP Passport, please give them a new one. Patients can have multiple BP Passports, they will all work correctly.

Find the correct patient

How to find patients

When you search for a patient by name or mobile number, it is important to select the correct patient.

Choose the correct patient

In the search results, it is very important to choose the correct patient. Always check:

  1. Name matches
  2. Age is correct
  3. Address is where the patient lives
  4. Mobile number matches
  5. Last visit date was a day the patient visited the hospital.

If the patient does not match any of the patients in the search, please register them as a new patient.

%{help_tip_svg}

Tip

If BP Passport scanning is not working, you can still type the BP Passport number into Simple on the same screen where you scan.

%{icon_back_svg}

Record a patient's visit

First, find the patient

You can find patients two ways: scan their BP Passport or search for the patient by name or mobile number.

Record BP and medicines

  1. On the patient's summary screen, press + Blood pressure
  2. Then, add patient's hypertension medicines.
  3. Select the correct medicines and their dosages. To remove a medicine, tap the dosage and select None
  4. Press Save and select the next visit date. Optional: You can change where the patient should visit next by pressing ‘Change’ below the patient’s current facility.
  5. Done. The patient visit is saved.
%{help_tip_svg}

Tips

  • You can select the next visit date on a calendar by tapping CHANGE when scheduling the visit.
  • If you select NOT NOW, the patient will not have a scheduled visit.
  • You can enter a BP from a previous visit. Enter the BP like normal and CHANGE the date.

Call overdue patients

What is "Overdue"?

Patients are overdue if they did not have a BP recorded by their scheduled visit date.

Patients at the top of the overdue patients list, are the most critical. Start calling patients at the top of the overdue list.

Calling patients

Call patients by pressing the phone icon. You may see two options: Normal call and Secure call. A "secure" call is toll-free will hide your phone number from the patient.

%{help_calling_svg}

Mark "result of call"

Please mark the result of each phone call that you make. Press on the patient card to see the following options:

  1. Agreed to visit if the patients tells you that they are coming for a follow-up.
  2. Remind to call later... if the patient didn’t mention when they can follow-up or didn’t answer their phone call.
  3. Remove from overdue list if:
    • Patient has already visited
    • Patient is not responding
    • Phone number does not work
    • Transferred to another facility
    • Moved to private practitioner
    • Died

Common questions

What should I do if my app crashes?

First try restarting your device. If Simple is crashing and you cannot fix it, please go to the Help screen and contact your supervisor or our team.

Scan isn't working. What should I do?

Enter the BP Passport number at the bottom of the screen. If the problem persists, restart your phone. If it still does not work, contact your supervisor.

How do I update the app?

Update the app by pressing the settings icon and pressing Update. It is important to have the latest version of Simple on your device.

%{icon_settings_svg}
I cannot find a follow-up patient

Sometimes it is difficult to find a follow-up patient when they do not have a BP Passport. If, after 5 minutes, you cannot find the patient, register them as a new patient. It is possible to combine the patient records in the future.

Can a patient have 2 BP Passports?

Yes. A patient can have multiple BP Passports. They will all continue to work.

I ran out of BP Passports

Please ask your supervisor to order more BP Passports, before you run out. Remember, you can still record patients even if you do not have any BP Passports — you can assign BP Passports later.

What is a "sync"?

A "sync" saves your data to a central server. Please make sure that your data gets saved at least once per day. If you do not have a good internet connection, your data might take some time to sync.

%{help_sync_svg}
How much data does Simple use?

Simple uses very little data. On average about 20 MB is used each month.

Can Simple access my information?

Simple does not access any of the personal information on your phone or tablet.
